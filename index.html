Cookie Exporter

Enter a URL to download its cookies as a .csv file.

How It Works

This tool uses a server-side automated browser to visit the URL you provide. It captures all accessible cookies (primarily first-party cookies due to modern browser privacy standards) and formats them into a downloadable CSV file. The process is ideal for developers needing to inspect session data or analysts reviewing a site's cookie implementation.
